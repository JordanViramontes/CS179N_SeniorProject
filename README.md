# Proximal

**Proximal** is a fast-paced movement FPS arena shooter where you wield the **Sinner’s Hand**—an arsenal of five leveling weapons tied to each of your character’s fingers. You must survive endless waves of grotesque celestial enemies drawn from the ranks of Heaven itself.

There is no end. The suffering will continue.

---

## Game Overview

- **Genre:** First-Person Arena Shooter
- **Core Loop:** Survive endless waves of increasingly difficult enemies while dynamically switching between your five weapons to maintain power and survivability.
- **Theme:** Heaven as a twisted battlefield. Beautifully grotesque angels. Celestial horror.

---

**Why Godot 4?**

We chose to develop in Godot 4 for its powerful and lightweight open-source game engine, perfect for fast-paced FPS development within the scope of our project. Key reasons for choosing it:

- **Scene-Based Design**: Modular, node-based scene system is ideal for weapon-swapping and layered abilities
- **Visual Scripting & GDScript**: Easy to iterate and prototype with a syntax similar to Python
- **Built-in Physics**: Includes great support for character controllers and custom physics interactions
- **Cross-Platform**: Builds run on PC, Web, and more—ideal for deployment to anywhere
